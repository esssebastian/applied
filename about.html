From Pixels to Intelligence: Our Evolution Story

Every great transformation starts with a simple beginning.

In 2018, Applied AI Networks Inc opened its doors in Vancouver as a lean, focused landing page and web design studio. Our mission was straightforward: help businesses turn their online presence into actual results.

We built beautiful pages that converted. Clean designs that worked. Websites that didn't just look good—they performed.

But as we worked with client after client, we kept hearing the same frustration: "The website looks great, but we're drowning in everything that happens after someone clicks."

That's when we realized we were solving the wrong problem.

2022: Going Deeper Than Design

A beautiful landing page means nothing if your team can't handle the leads it generates. A sleek website doesn't help if your operations can't scale with your growth.

So in 2022, we made a pivot that changed everything.

We stopped being just a design studio and became a custom software company. We started building the systems behind the websites—the automation, the workflows, the infrastructure that actually runs a business.

CRM integrations. Automated lead qualification. Custom booking systems. Data pipelines that eliminated manual entry.

Our clients weren't just getting prettier websites anymore. They were getting smarter operations.

The feedback was immediate: "You just gave us back 20 hours a week." "We can finally scale without hiring three more people." "This is what we actually needed."

We'd found our real calling—building technology that eliminates operational bottlenecks.

2025: The AI Revolution We Couldn't Ignore

Then came the AI explosion of 2024-2025.

Suddenly, what used to require months of custom development could be built in weeks. What once needed expensive infrastructure could run intelligently in the cloud. What seemed impossible yesterday became standard practice today.

But here's what we noticed: most businesses had no idea how to actually use any of it.

They were drowning in AI hype but starving for practical implementation. They knew automation could help but didn't know where to start. They heard about voice agents and intelligent systems but couldn't connect the dots to their actual problems.

So we evolved again.

In 2025, Applied AI Networks Inc became what we are today: an intelligent automation company specializing in AI-powered systems, voice agents, and custom software that actually solves real business problems.

We don't chase trends. We build solutions.

2025: Expanding Across the Americas

This year also marks our geographic expansion.

After seven years serving North American businesses from Vancouver, we opened our LATAM headquarters in Bogotá, Colombia.

Why? Because Latin American businesses are at the perfect inflection point. They're growing rapidly, embracing technology, and ready to compete globally—but they need partners who understand their markets, speak their language, and build solutions for their unique challenges.

Our Bogotá team brings the same problem-solving approach we've honed in Vancouver, adapted for the dynamic markets across Latin America.
